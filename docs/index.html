About Me

My name is Evan Nishi, and I'm a freshman at Las Lomas High School. I have always been curious and loved challenges, this personality led me to love math, physics, electronics, and programming. I have been programming for a little over a year now, and it has become my favorite hobby. I have been building a bunch of project recently, and you can check them out on my github! I prefer back end programming, and have a love for machine learning, although I am willing to do front end work. Right now I am mainly exploring ruby gems and doing machine learning applications with python. I am very active in the programming and local community. I fell in love with the programming community after my first hackathon at HackMerced which I won best beginner prize. After HackMerced I wanted to help the community, but didn't know how until I attended Los Altos hacks (which my team won best android app). Ever since then I could not stop coding and helping the community in any way I can. I spread my passion of learning and programming by organizing, attending, and volunteering for events. Hope to see you around!
